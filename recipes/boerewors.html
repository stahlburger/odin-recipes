Recipe description

First of you need a stove and you need money to buy the ingredients. After that you need to get n girlfriend or a wife. You will also need to purchase an apron.

You will need a fire extinguisher and a fire pool within easy access from the kitchen. Start off by setting the oven to 1000 degrees celsius.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta pieces
  • Lean mince meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Garlic and herb spice
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Pan fry mince
  2. Toss in tomato sauce
  3. Add tablespoon sugar and salt
  4. Let in cook for 8 minutes
  5. Add 2l warm water to a pot and bring to a boil
  6. Add lasagna pieces to boiling water and remove after 3 minutes
  7. Place all the shit in a container and put in oven for 30 minutes
